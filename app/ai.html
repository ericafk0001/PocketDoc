Example guidance

Clean the area gently, keep it dry, and avoid using harsh products unless a clinician told you to.

Cover it with a clean dressing if it is open or likely to get dirty.

Get urgent help if redness spreads quickly, pain gets worse, pus appears, or you have a fever.
